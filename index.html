In this webpage we are demonstrating JavaScript's "Date" object, conditionals like a "switch" block, and linking URL parameters. To rotate content I used data extracted from the Date object and then used a conditional to choose which HTML is appropriate to show. Since there are seven days there will be seven different drink specials. This will also mean that not only will the drink change, but the page will change 7 different times. Depending on the day the page will change the image of the drink the background of the page and the description of the drink..
